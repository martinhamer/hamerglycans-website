Community Self-Experiment

The Two-Week Trigger Trial

Run a compressed version of the experiment that started all of this — on yourself, in two weeks.

Cohort 1: Monday, July 13 – Thursday, July 30, 2026
Limited to the first 150 registrants, then a waitlist.

Twenty-three years ago, one accidental dietary change was the first data point behind everything on this site. This trial lets you test the same pattern in your own life: a short, structured period of avoidance, one clearly defined reintroduction, and an honest look at what changes. You don't need to believe the hypothesis to take part — only to be willing to observe your own body and report it accurately.

This isn't a replacement for anything you're already doing. It runs alongside your current treatment, and every result — including “nothing changed” — is useful data.

Eligibility

Who it's for

This round is for people with moderate to severe, persistent acne, rosacea, or excessively oily skin — present most days, not just occasional breakouts. If your skin is currently clear or only mildly affected, there won't be enough of a signal to make the two weeks worthwhile, so we'd ask you to sit this cohort out.

The Protocol

What's involved

  1. Day 0: A baseline photo and a simple 0–10 severity rating
  2. Days 1–14: Avoid the major Neu5Gc sources (red meat, dairy) and ATI/gliadin sources (wheat), using easy substitutions
  3. Day 14: A second assessment, same conditions as Day 0
  4. Day 15: One clearly defined reintroduction meal
  5. Days 15–17: Track what happens in the 6-hour and 24-hour windows that follow
Safety

Before you join

This is not a clinical trial and not medical advice. Talk to your doctor or healthcare provider before starting, especially if you're pregnant or breastfeeding, have a history of disordered eating, take medication affected by diet, or are currently being treated for the condition you're hoping to see change in. Full eligibility guidance is in the protocol.
